Project timelines vary based on scope. Simple websites take 2-4 weeks, while animation projects may take 4-12 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Yes! We include revision rounds in all our packages. We work collaboratively with you to ensure the final product meets your expectations.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, online payments, and can work with various payment arrangements. We typically require a deposit before starting work.

Absolutely! We offer maintenance packages and ongoing support for all our projects. We're committed to long-term partnerships with our clients.
