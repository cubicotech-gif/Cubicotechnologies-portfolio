## ğŸ“ **IMAGE OPTIMIZATION TIPS**

### **Before Uploading:**

1. **Resize Images**
   - Use recommended dimensions above
   - Don't upload 4K images if you only need 1080p

2. **Compress Images**
   - Use online tools:
     - **TinyPNG**: https://tinypng.com (PNG/JPG)
     - **SVGOMG**: https://jakearchibald.github.io/svgomg/ (SVG)
     - **Squoosh**: https://squoosh.app (All formats)

3. **Convert to WebP** (optional, for best performance)
   - Modern format, smaller file sizes
   - Use: https://convertio.co/webp-converter/

### **Target File Sizes:**
- Logo: Under 50KB
- Team photos: Under 200KB
- Portfolio: Under 500KB
- Backgrounds: Under 1MB
